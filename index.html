📚 Excelsior Library Rules & Regulations

Issue Duration

  • Books must be returned within 30 days from the date of issue.
  • Late return may attract a fine as per library policy.

Late Fine Policy

  • ₹10 per day fine after the due date (can be modified as per your club decision).
  • Continued delay may lead to suspension of borrowing privileges.

Book Condition

  • Handle books with care. Do not write, underline, tear, or damage pages.
  • If a book is damaged or lost, the borrower must replace the book or pay the full cost.

Approval Process

  • Book issue requests must be approved by the Library Admin before confirmation.
  • Issue will be considered valid only after admin approval.

Responsibility Clause

  • Members must follow all library rules strictly.
  • The library committee reserves the right to modify rules when required.
